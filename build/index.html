About NFTS Wearables

where freedom blooms..

We are a designer phygital (physical + digital) brand inspired by Trinidad and Tobago Carnival and Burning Man, aiming to empower women to experience true freedom and radical self expression, while affording financial independence to the women who manufacture the line.

How do we do it?

WYLD FLWR has partnered with women's livelihood initiative, Spöol, to ensure that fair, living wages are paid to our makers and commit a percentage of royalties earned through the sale of our digital assets to Spöol.
