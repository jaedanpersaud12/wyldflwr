About Us

Wyld Flwr is a designer fashion brand that integrates festival fashion and culture with innovative blockchain technology. Our first capsule collection, "Wyld Ting", features an inspiring collaboration of creative and tech professionals in the form of nun-fungible tokens (NFTs). This hybrid offering, unique to the Caribbean region, will be released on the curated NFT marketplace Foundation in three drops: Origin, Enchantress and Her, and will guide collectors through the journey of the Wyld woman both visually and aurally...
